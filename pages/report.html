Filing business

Reported party

Review details

Describe the marketplace issue and the kind of resolution or status update you are seeking.

Supporting material

Mask account numbers, identity numbers, and unrelated personal information before uploading documents.

Before you file

Required
  • The matter must relate to a marketplace transaction or attempted transaction.
  • Use observable facts tied to the event, not insults or speculation.
  • Do not include medical, identity, or unrelated financial details.
  • Supporting records may be requested before publication review is completed.

How reviews are handled

Typical path
  • Within 2 business days Accepted filings move into bureau notice or document review.
  • Within 14 days The reported party is typically asked to respond.
  • Within 30 days Many review matters are updated, published, or closed.

Publication note

Public summary only

Public files show a screened summary. CRB does not publish full uploads, raw account records, or internal handling notes.
