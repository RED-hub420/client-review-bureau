Before you file

Required
  • Use observable facts tied to the transaction.
  • Do not file threats, insults, or speculation as part of the summary.
  • Do not include medical, identity, or unrelated financial details.
  • Files may be screened before they are handled or published.

Handling path

Reference
  • Received The bureau logs the filing and checks it against submission standards.
  • Accepted for handling The file qualifies for review and may move into notice or publication review.
  • Response period The other party may be contacted and allowed to respond.
  • Published or closed The file is published, limited, withdrawn, or closed based on review outcome.
